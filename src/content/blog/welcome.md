We're building ViFi because we believe small business owners deserve better than voicemail.

When your phone rings and you can't answer, most callers don't leave a message. They call the next business. And when someone does leave a voicemail, you call back with no real context — spending the first few minutes figuring out what they need instead of helping them.

## What ViFi does differently

ViFi is an AI phone assistant that answers your calls with a natural, professional voice. But answering the call is just the beginning.

After every conversation, ViFi delivers what we call **post-call intelligence**: a concise summary of the call, the full word-for-word transcript, and a recording. You get notified instantly — by text, email, or both.

When you call back, you already know who called, what they need, and what to say. No guessing, no awkward "remind me what you called about."

## Who we built this for

ViFi is for small business owners and professionals who depend on the phone to drive revenue:

- **Home service contractors** who miss calls while they're on a job
- **Medical practices** with front desks overwhelmed by call volume
- **Law firms** where a single missed intake call can cost thousands
- **Real estate agents** who can't answer while showing homes

If your phone is a revenue-generating tool and you can't always be there to answer it, ViFi is built for you.
