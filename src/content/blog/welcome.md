We're building ViFi because small business owners deserve better than voicemail.

When your phone rings and you can't answer, most callers don't leave a message. They call the next business. And when someone does leave a voicemail, you call back with zero context, spending the first few minutes piecing together what they need instead of helping them.

## What ViFi does differently

ViFi is an AI phone assistant that answers your calls with a professional voice. But picking up is just the start.

After every conversation, ViFi sends you a call recap: a short summary, the word-for-word transcript, and a recording. You get notified instantly by text, email, or both.

When you call back, you already know who it was, what they need, and what to say. No guessing, no awkward "remind me what you called about."

## Who we built this for

ViFi is for small business owners and professionals whose phone drives revenue:

- **Home service contractors** who miss calls on the job site
- **Medical practices** with front desks buried in call volume
- **Law firms** where one missed intake call can cost thousands
- **Real estate agents** who can't answer during showings

If your phone is a revenue tool and you can't always be there to answer it, ViFi is for you.
